About us

We are a family of 4. We have 5 dogs, 3 cats, 2 donkeys, 7 goats (3 billies and 4 ladies), 8 chickens, and 3 ducks!

We live on a small plot of only 5 acres of land.

We love to teach about caring for animals and the ways in which you too can be more sustainable on your small plot of land!
